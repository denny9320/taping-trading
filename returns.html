退换货政策 / Returns & Exchanges Policy

适用对象：本政策适用于所有批发（B2B）和定制（OEM/ODM）订单。

Policy Scope: This policy applies to all wholesale (B2B) and custom (OEM/ODM) orders.

一、退货资格 / Return Eligibility

在以下情况下，您有资格申请退货：

You are eligible for a return if:

  • 收到商品后 30天内 发现质量问题或制造缺陷（功能性缺陷）
  • 收到的商品与确认的规格严重不符（尺寸、材质、功能、标准等）
  • 发货错误（发错型号、配置、数量超出公差范围）
  • 运输过程中包装破损或商品损坏（需在收货后48小时内报告）
  • Within 30 days of receipt for quality issues or manufacturing defects (functional defects)
  • Product materially deviates from confirmed specifications (size, material, function, standards, etc.)
  • Wrong item shipped (wrong model, configuration, quantity beyond tolerance)
  • Visible damage or shortage during transit (report within 48 hours of delivery)

❌ 不可退货的情况 / Non-Returnable Items

  • 定制产品（OEM/ODM）：已印制Logo、特殊颜色、定制包装、特殊规格的产品，除非存在功能性质量问题，否则不接受退货。
  • 清仓/特价商品：除非另有书面约定，否则清仓商品视为最终销售，不接受退货。
  • 买家原因：因买家改变主意、订购错误、未支付关税等原因，不接受退货（除非商品存在质量问题）。
  • 已拆改商品：买家自行拆解、改装、维修或物理改动的产品，不接受退货。
  • Custom Products (OEM/ODM): Products customized with your logo, specific colors, or unique packaging cannot be returned unless functionally defective.
  • Clearance Items: Unless otherwise agreed in writing, clearance items are final sale.
  • Buyer's Remorse: Change of mind, ordering errors, or refusal to pay customs duties are not eligible for return.
  • Tampered Items: Products disassembled, modified, or altered by the buyer are not eligible for return.

二、退货流程 / Return Process (RMA System)

第1步：联系客服 / Step 1: Contact Support

在收货后 7天内，通过以下方式联系我们的客服团队：

Within 7 days of delivery, contact our support team:

  • 邮箱 / Email: kyx9320@163.com
  • WhatsApp: +86 133 9765 5758 或 / or +86 191 8670 6454
  • 提供订单号、退货原因、以及清晰的照片/视频证据
  • Provide order number, reason for return, and clear photo/video evidence

第2步：获取RMA授权 / Step 2: Get RMA Authorization

我们的客服团队将在 24-48小时内 审核您的申请，并通过邮件发送：

Our support team will review your request within 24-48 hours and provide via email:

  • 退货授权号（RMA Number）
  • 退货地址和包装指引
  • Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  • Return address and packing instructions

第3步：寄回商品 / Step 3: Ship the Return

在收到RMA后 14天内 寄回商品：

Ship the item back within 14 days of receiving RMA:

  • 使用可追踪的物流服务（DHL、FedEx、UPS等）
  • 在包裹外清晰标注RMA号码
  • 保留运单号作为凭证
  • 商品需包含全部配件和原包装
  • Use a trackable shipping service (DHL, FedEx, UPS, etc.)
  • Clearly mark the RMA number on the package
  • Keep the tracking number as proof
  • Item must include all accessories and original packaging

⚠️ 海关申报：国际退货时，请在包裹上标注"Returned Goods – No Commercial Value"以避免关税。如因申报不当产生关税，将从您的退款中扣除。

⚠️ Customs Declaration: For international returns, mark the parcel as "Returned Goods – No Commercial Value" to avoid duties. If duties occur due to incorrect declaration, they will be deducted from your refund.

第4步：验收与退款 / Step 4: Inspection & Refund

我们的仓库收到退货后，将进行验收：

After our warehouse receives your return, we will inspect:

  • 验收通过后，将在 5-10个工作日 内处理退款
  • 退款将原路返回至您的原支付方式
  • 您的银行/支付提供商可能需要额外 5-15个工作日 完成入账
  • Refund will be processed within 5-10 business days after inspection
  • Refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • Your bank/payment provider may take an additional 5-15 business days to post the credit

⚠️ 费用扣除：

  • 原始运费（如有）不予退还，除非退货原因是我们的错误
  • 如缺少配件、包装破损或商品被使用/损坏，将合理扣除相应费用
  • 银行手续费、支付处理费（如PayPal费用）和汇率波动损失不予退还
  • Cost Deductions: Original shipping fees (if any) are non-refundable unless return is due to our error. Missing accessories or damaged packaging may result in reasonable deductions. Bank fees, payment processing fees, and currency exchange losses are non-refundable.

Due to the nature of B2B wholesale and custom orders, we do not offer direct exchanges.

如需更换商品，请：

If you need a replacement, please:

  • 先按上述流程退回原商品并获得退款
  • 然后重新下单购买所需商品
  • 这样可确保更快的处理速度和准确的库存管理
  • First return the original item and receive refund following the process above
  • Then place a new order for the desired item
  • This ensures faster processing and accurate inventory management
