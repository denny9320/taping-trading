退换政策 / Returns & Exchanges

At TapingTrading, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, we offer a simple and hassle-free return and exchange policy.

Note: This policy applies to wholesale orders. For retail customers, please contact us directly for assistance.

1. Return Eligibility

  • Products must be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be in their original condition - unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.
  • Custom/OEM products are not eligible for return unless defective.
  • Proof of purchase (order number) is required.

2. Exchange Process

  • Contact our customer service at service@tapingtrading.com within 30 days of receipt.
  • Provide your order number and reason for exchange.
  • We will provide a return shipping address and RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) number.
  • Ship the item back to us with the RMA number clearly marked on the package.

3. Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days after we receive the returned items.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (defective/damaged items).

4. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged product:

  • Contact us within 7 days of receipt with photos of the damage.
  • We will arrange a replacement or full refund at our expense.
  • Do not return the item before receiving our confirmation.
