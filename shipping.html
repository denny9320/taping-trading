At TapingTrading, we strive to deliver your orders quickly and safely. Below are our shipping policies and timelines.

Note: This policy applies to wholesale orders. For retail customers, please contact us directly for assistance.

1. Shipping Methods & Costs

2. Order Processing Time

  • In-stock items: 1-3 business days
  • Custom/OEM orders: 15-25 days (as specified in product details)
  • Bulk orders (500+ pieces): 20-30 days

3. Tracking & Notifications

  • You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.
  • Track your order at: DHL | FedEx | UPS
  • Contact service@tapingtrading.com for shipping inquiries.

4. Customs & Duties

  • Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.
  • We declare items as "samples" or "gifts" upon request (wholesale only).
  • We are not responsible for delays due to customs clearance.

5. Lost or Damaged Shipments

  • Report lost/damaged shipments within 7 days of expected delivery.
  • We will file claims with the carrier and arrange replacements.
  • Provide photos of damaged packaging/items for faster processing.
