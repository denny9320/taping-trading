隐私条款 / Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how TapingTrading ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website.

Effective Date: May 1, 2026

Last Updated: May 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email, name, phone number, shipping addresses
  • Order Information: Products purchased, quantities, total amounts
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe (we do not store card details)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, products viewed, time spent on site

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate order updates and shipping notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send promotional emails (you can opt-out anytime)
  • Improve our website and product offerings
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (authentication, database), Stripe (payments), Formspree (newsletter)
  • Shipping Partners: DHL, FedEx, UPS (for order delivery)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

4. Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (Firebase authentication)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (you can opt-out via browser settings)
  • Marketing Cookies: Facebook Pixel (for ad retargeting)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS encryption
  • Firebase Authentication for secure user management
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and employee training

6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Order history: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Marketing data: Retained until you opt-out

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or website notice.
